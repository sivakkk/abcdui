1. For Developer - OCLAVI provides a platform where developers can annotate objects either by themselves, by their team or choose OCLAVI team to annotate. Thus, enabling developer to concentrate much on developing model.

2. For Business entity or entrepreneurs – OCLAVI helps them bring their team and complete their projects in most economical way.
