GDPR Compliance
What is GDPR?
The EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is widespread data protection law that addresses on data protection and privacy of all individuals within the European Union (EU). This regulation provides single set of rules to control the processing and monitoring of EU data.

How is OCLAVI preparing for GDPR?
At OCLAVI (Carabiner Technologies Private Limited), we build very strong data protection framework for all our users and also, we ensure that we are meeting the obligations and taking appropriate actions for our users.

We have updated our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service to maintain transparency and make users understand what information we gather and why do we collect it.

We have dynamic navigation which helps users to easily find out what you are looking for.

As we use certain third party contractors to make OCLAVI available, we maintain DPA (Data Processing Agreements) Standards.

We shall keep updating the information from time to time, for further queries kindly contact support@oclavi.com
